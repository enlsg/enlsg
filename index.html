Shout Out on Comm!

Feel free to message us on the in-game comm should you want to join our community. Here is how:

1. In the main game screen, tap on the main menu (white circle) at the bottom of the screen.

2. Tap on the Comm menu to launch the communication screen.

3. Tap on the CHAT on top, and make sure Faction is highlighted, then tap on the keyboard icon at the lower right of the screen to initiate the chat. All of us (Enlightened) will then be able to see your message.

If you need to alert/reply an agent, you should add their agent name in the message. For example, to reply to an agent named "agent123", simply include @agent123 before your actual message.
